Forjando Historia

In 2016, Dr. Curipoma, together with his family, headed a health project that would revolutionize medical care in the city by implementing the most important health project in the province with a much more complete infrastructure, providing comfort and safety to patients. This property was subsequently acquired by the Ministry of Public Health.
In 2019, the Curipoma family and the Mendoza family started a new health project located in the center of Santo Domingo: Hospital General Clínica Santiago.
Today we have the satisfaction of offering our medical services with state-of-the-art technology and the most complete medical staff in
Santo Domingo and the region. Over the years, the objectives have been met, which have resulted in constant growth and innovation

  • Misión Ofrecer los mejores servicios de salud en prevención, rehabilitación y recuperación con seguridad, eficacia y calidez.
  • Visión Ser líderes regionales en servicios de salud certificados, a través de la excelencia profesional, tecnología moderna y mejora continua
